A Substitution Cipher

abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz

xgfvwuniepoqdbcrkmzhaytljs

hello world

iwqqc tcmqv

This is a terrible cipher!

Take each letter and substitute it with another We encrypt the phrase "hello world" with this cipher This is a terrible cipher - Which letters often appear in a pair in the middle of a word? g, l, m, n, p, s, t - What words are likely to open a communication? hi, hello
